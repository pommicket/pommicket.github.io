AutoVideos

AutoVideos is a program that creates computer generated videos.
For an explanation of AutoAudio, go here First click the create button then the download button.
If the download button doesn't work, just right click on the image then click "Save image as...". Change X and Y to change the resolution of the image.
After you click create, wait until the image shows, then click "Download".
